Crop Yield Prediction FAQs

What data do I need to input? ▼

You need to input data such as soil type, weather conditions, and crop variety.

How accurate are the predictions? ▼

The predictions are highly accurate, with an error margin of less than 5%.

Can I use it for any crop? ▼

Yes, the tool supports predictions for a wide range of crops.

Is it user-friendly for farmers? ▼

If you can use a smartphone, you’re golden!

What if I input wrong data? ▼

The tool will guide you through correcting the data input.
